Welcome!

1) Press on 'left-arrow' & 'right-arrow' & 'up-arrow' & 'down-arrow' to step left & right & forward & backward.

2) Press on 'A' & 'D' & 'W' & 'S' to look left & right & up & down.

3) Press on 'J' & 'L' to turn left & right while keeping moving.

4) Press on '1' & '2' & '3' & '4' to change the frustum parameters in order to reset the orthographic camera.

5) Click & drag mouse to rotate the first left tree and the robot head with quanternion-based ‘trackball’ control.

6) Press on 'C' to switch Perspective camera to show view from the hand of the robot. Press again to change back to the original perspective camera.

7) ‘Flying-airplane’ navigation controls: First press on 'U' to set a constant forward velocity. Then press on 'M' & 'N' to pitch. Or Press on 'left-arrow' & 'right-arrow' to turn left & right. Or Press on 'W' & 'S' to fly up and down. If you want to stop, just press 'U' again.
